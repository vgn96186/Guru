Guru Shell Directions

All three directions keep the same core ingredients: simple Home, top-level Tree, top-level Vault, top-level Stats, floating Guru chat, and lecture capture on Home.

Launchpad + Tree

Home is extremely simple. Tree is the strategic destination. Best for reducing friction and decision fatigue.

B. Adaptive Home
Exam countdown + state slider
Smart Start card
Lecture capture
Today's path + recommended rescue
Tree still top-level, but home adapts more

Adaptive Home

Home does a bit more thinking in public. Stronger GuruBrain presence, but slightly higher surface complexity.

C. Tree-Centric Product
Condensed home
Tree preview embedded on home
Launch into active branch
The digital tree dominates the product identity

Tree-Centric Product

Most distinctive and memorable, but risks making the app feel heavier when you just need to start quickly.

Recommendation

  • A is my recommendation.
  • It protects your requirement that Home stay uncluttered.
  • It still gives the tree a major top-level role instead of burying it.

Trade-offs

  • B may overthink the home screen.
  • C is exciting but could hurt bad-day usability.
